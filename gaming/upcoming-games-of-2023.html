Games of 2023

In 2023 Several game companies announced their games This might be the list of games you would like to play.

1. Starfield : 

Starfield is an action and role-playing game developed and Published by Bethesda Game Studios and Bethesda Softworks. It is announced in 2018 at Bethesda's E3 Presentation. It is set to release on 6 September 2023 on Xbox and PC. The cost of making Starfield is estimated to be 200 $ Million

2. The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom :

The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom is a sequel to The Legend of Zelda  Breath of Wild which was launched in 2017. The game was Developed and Published by Nintendo and Nintendo Switch This game sits in a new land and is said to be launched in 2022 but for some reason, it is delayed to May 2023. The estimated cost of the game is said to be 75$ Million. The game already sold Millions of copies in the First 2 Weeks. It is available on Nintendo.

3. Hogwarts Legacy : 

Hogwarts Legacy is an action role-playing game That takes place in the Late 1800s in the Harry Potter novels. It is developed and published by Avalanche Software and Warner Bros Games under its Portkey Games label. It's a single-player player game one can enjoy on Windows, Xbox, PlayStation and Nintendo. It was released on 10 February, 5 May 2023 for different platforms, Made at a cost of 150 $ Million.
